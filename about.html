Our Story

Born from a Spark of Hope

Kalyaan Ujwala Foundation was established with a simple belief: every human being deserves dignity, education, and care. Ujwala means light — our work brings that light into the darkest corners of society.

What began as volunteers distributing food has grown into a multi-program NGO impacting thousands through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and social welfare.

Mission

To serve underprivileged communities through sustainable programs in education, health, women empowerment, and social support.

Vision

A compassionate world where every individual lives with hope, health, knowledge, and self-respect.
