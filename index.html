Who We Are

Lighting Lives with Compassion

Kalyaan Ujwala Foundation is dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and social support.

Founded with a vision to spread the light of hope (Ujwala), our foundation works tirelessly across rural and urban communities to create lasting change for the most vulnerable.

Every donation, every volunteer hour, and every partnership helps us reach more families in need — because together, we can change lives.

Our Mission

To empower women, educate children, and provide medical & social support to underprivileged communities with dignity and love.

Our Vision

A society where every child learns, every woman thrives, and every family receives the care and opportunity they deserve.

Our Work

Programs That Transform Lives

From classrooms to clinics, our initiatives reach the heart of communities in need.

Women Empowerment

Skill training, self-help groups, and livelihood support to help women become financially independent.

Education

Free schooling, books, uniforms, and scholarships for poor children who dream of a brighter tomorrow.

Medical Support

Free health camps, medicines, and emergency aid for underprivileged families who cannot afford treatment.

Food Distribution

Monthly ration kits and community kitchens ensuring no family sleeps hungry during difficult times.
